Kumaoni Studio

Kamla Devi is a torchbearer of Kumaoni folk music, carrying forward the rich musical heritage of Uttarakhand with her powerful and soulful voice. Born in the heart of the Kumaon region, she grew up immersed in the melodies of her ancestors. Songs that celebrate nature, everyday life, and deep-rooted traditions. Her music reflects the spirit of the mountains, echoing the voices of her people and keeping alive the age-old folklore passed down through generations.

With modernization pushing many traditional art forms to the brink of extinction, Kamla Devi has taken it upon herself to preserve and revive Kumaoni folk music. Through her performances, she not only entertains but educates, ensuring that the cultural essence of her homeland continues to thrive. Her songs narrate tales of love, devotion, and the struggles of mountain life, making her a voice for the people of Kumaon.

Her collaboration with Coke Studio Bharat has brought Kumaoni music to a larger audience, bridging the gap between tradition and modernity. By presenting her folk melodies on a national platform, she is helping revive interest in the music of her community, ensuring that it reaches younger generations who may have never heard these timeless tunes before.
